AI Tools Guide for EPFO Staff

A curated guide on how to leverage specific Artificial Intelligence tools to enhance productivity, research, and content creation in our daily operations.

Popular LLM Chatbots

A summarized list of popular AI assistants and their specialized use cases (what separates them from others):

  • ChatGPT: The most well-known general-purpose conversational AI. Best for coding, deep data analysis, canvas writing workspaces, and creating Custom GPTs trained on your instructions.
  • Claude: Excels in highly nuanced, human-like writing and analyzing incredibly long documents. Known for producing writing that feels less "robotic" than other models.
  • Gemini: Google's natively multimodal model. Fast internet searches, excellent integration with Google Workspace (Docs, Gmail, Drive), and accepts video/audio inputs natively.
  • Google AI Studio (aistudio.google.com): A developer platform, but incredibly useful for non-tech users as it offers free access to the most powerful Pro versions of Gemini with very high usage limits and no chat guardrails.
  • Perplexity: An AI answer engine replacing traditional search. Instead of providing links, it reads web pages and gives you an exact answer with clickable citations for deep internet research.
  • Grok: X's (formerly Twitter) AI model. Its major separator is direct real-time access to the live X post stream, making it the best for current news and trending events.

Deep Research & Document Intelligence

  • NotebookLM: Upload your own PDFs, links, or text. The AI answers questions based strictly on your materials. Perfect for finding information in heavy manuals, summarizing reports into PPT bullet points, and uniquely generating realistic, multi-lingual "podcast-style" audio discussions based on the material.

Design & Imagery

  • Remove.bg: Instantly removes the background from any image perfectly with a single click. No Photoshop skills required.
  • Canva: The ultimate drag-and-drop design tool for posters, flyers, and presentations with built-in AI image generation.
  • Freepik: Download high-quality stock photos, vectors, and use their AI image generator to bring ideas to life.

Audio & Video Presentations

  • Gamma.app: AI presentation maker. Type a topic and it generates a fully formatted, beautifully designed PowerPoint deck instantly.
  • Adobe Podcast Enhance: Free tool to drop in a noisy voice recording and instantly process it to sound like a professional studio.
  • ElevenLabs: The premier AI voice generation tool for realistic, emotive human text-to-speech across multiple languages.
  • Suno AI: Type a text description of a song and it writes lyrics, generates music, and produces a professional-sounding track.

Writing, PDF & Utilities

  • QuillBot: An excellent paraphrasing tool to rewrite emails or reports to sound more professional, fluent, or concise.
  • DeepL Translator: Highly accurate AI-driven translation tool that significantly outperforms standard tools in formal language.
  • Ilovepdf.com: Must-have toolkit to merge, split, compress, or convert PDFs without any heavy desktop software.
  • Goblin.tools: Breaks large tasks into small, manageable checklists and judges the tone of text.

AI App & Web Builders (Prompt-to-Software)

  • Google Stitch: Google's revolutionary new tool. You describe an interface or upload a sketch, and it instantly generates a fully responsive UI layout with components and color schemes. Brilliant for rapid visual prototyping.
  • v0.dev (by Vercel): The absolute standard for UI generation. Simply type "a modern dashboard for tracking claims" and it instantly writes the working interface code in front of your eyes.
  • Lovable.dev & Bolt.new: These tools don't just build the design; they build the entire software application. You can type instructions and deploy a fully working, database-connected website in 5 minutes with zero engineering experience.
  • Claude Artifacts: Inside the standard Claude.ai chat, if you ask it to build a web tool, a game, or a dashboard, an "Artifact" window opens on the right side where the app runs live instantly.

Development & Data Science

  • Google Colab: Cloud-based Jupyter notebook environment. Write and execute Python directly in the browser with free GPU access.
  • Kaggle: The premier platform for data science competitions, hosting open datasets, and notebooks. Great for learning and collaboration.
  • GitHub: The industry standard for code version control and collaborative development. Essential for storing and sharing code repositories.

Flowcharts, Mind Maps & Visualization

  • Napkin AI: Paste in any text and it instantly auto-generates beautiful flowcharts, infographics, and cycle diagrams. (Magic visualizer).
  • Whimsical: The absolute fastest drag-and-drop tool for making mind maps, wireframes, and flowcharts. Includes an AI mind-map generator.
  • Draw.io (Diagrams.net): The completely free, standard offline tool. Great for government staff because it requires zero login and saves files securely to your own hard drive.
  • XMind: A powerful, dedicated mind-mapping tool for brainstorming and structuring complex thoughts and projects.

Learning AI & Data Basics (Beginners)

  • Elements of AI (Course): A brilliant free course created specifically for non-technical people to understand what AI is, what it can do, and what it can't.
  • IBM Technology - AI Explained: Excellent, bite-sized "whiteboard" videos answering simple questions like "What is Generative AI?" or "What is a Large Language Model?".
  • CrashCourse: Artificial Intelligence: A fun, highly visual, fast-paced YouTube series covering the absolute basics of ML, neural networks, and algorithmic bias.
  • "AI for Everyone" concepts (Andrew Ng): The globally recognized standard framework for non-technical staff to learn how to spot opportunities for AI.

Tutorials & Advanced Academics

  • Kevin Stratvert (YouTube): Former Microsoft PM providing the best step-by-step, screen-recorded tutorials on exactly how to use tools like ChatGPT, NotebookLM, and Copilot.
  • Jeff Su (YouTube): Highly valuable for office professionals. Teaches practical workflows like using AI to organize Gmail, draft meeting notes, and manage Google Workspace.
  • IIT Delhi "Artificial Intelligence" (Prof. Mausam): Advanced Academic Course: A highly rigorous, mathematical NPTEL course covering deep AI computer science concepts (A* search, logic, MDPs). Excellent for technical mastery, but not for everyday office use.
